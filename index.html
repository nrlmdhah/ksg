About Us

KSG SDN BHD

Kawalan Seri Gading Sdn Bhd (1101016-M) is a newly established, wholly-owned Bumiputera private limited company, incorporated under the Private Agency Act 1971. Our core business is delivering professional and private security services tailored to the needs of various industries. KSG also offers contractor services for building, office, and area cleaning. We supply food, beverages, raw materials, and freshly prepared halal meals in line with Islamic principles.


KSG, a newcomer in the security industry, is confident in the expertise and experience of its knowledgeable servicemen. With years of experience in security businesses, they aim to meet today's security needs. They assure their clients that their tenderfoot is in good hands, offering services to address security problems and protect investments, property, and loved ones. They emphasize that prevention is better than cure, ensuring protection from harm.

Our Mission

As one of the Security Agencies, we are entitled to provide total security services to our valued clients; by having skilled, experienced and knowledgeable staff to meet our clients’ needs, and by serving our clients with full respect, honesty and professionalism.

Our Vision

As a business-oriented organization, we are committed to offering efficient security services to our valued clients, while elevating the standards of the security industry.

Objective

Kawalan Seri Gading’s main core business is to provide security services to clients in need. To meet our clients’ satisfaction, Kawalan Seri Gading emphasizes the importance of delivering reliable, high-quality, and high-value services.

We are fully committed to providing effective security and professional investigative services. Our objectives are based on the following principles:

Provide high standard of professionalism in the services.

Ensure that our standard and quality of output reflects the good image and vast experience relating to our expertise.

Ensure the smooth operations of the company by adopting strict and rigorous HR policies, including attractive staff benefits, recruitment strategies, and an employment plan — enabling us to hire and retain top talent while providing career development paths.

Establish and carry out the business of security services with a focus on specialization.

Code of Performance

Job Interest

We employ young and matured guards, including ex-military and ex-police personnel. They are well-trained, motivated, and closely supervised. Good wages and career paths help us retain quality staff.

Discipline & Tact

Our guards are trained to be professional, patient, and disciplined. Positive thinking helps them make sound judgments while remaining calm and fair in all situations.

Alertness

Our guards are always alert, trained to notice any suspicious changes or risks. With prevention as a priority, they help protect people and property from harm or loss.

Courtesy

Friendly and respectful, our guards greet and interact with visitors professionally, creating a calm and welcoming environment that reflects your company’s image.
